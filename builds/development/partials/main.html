About YumDB

YumDB is a new kind of recipe database. Rather than searching just by keywords like most common recipe databases, YumDB is capable of searching by ingredients you want to include and exclude in your food. So save the trip to the grocery store, get cooking with YumDB using the ingredients you already have!

Waste less food

Always have trouble using food before it expires? Gather up all your food that's about to expire and find a recipe that incorporates most or all of those ingredients. You'll save time and money, and waste much less food!


Become the chef you've always imagined

Search for recipes based on allergies, dietary restrictions, specific courses, types of cuisine, and even holidays. Pick the ingredients you want to use and even the ingredients you don't, and pick an awesome recipe to impress your friends and family based on your exact specifications!
